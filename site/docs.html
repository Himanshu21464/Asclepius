Cryptographic primitives at a glance

  • BLAKE2b-25632-byte payload + entry hashes. libsodium crypto_generichash.
  • Ed2551964-byte detached signatures over the canonical sign-input.
  • Canonical JSONUTF-8, sorted keys, no whitespace. Bit-equal across hosts.
  • SQLite WALsingle-writer, many-reader; sidecar friendly.
  • USTAR tarevidence bundles open with tar xf, jq, openssl.
