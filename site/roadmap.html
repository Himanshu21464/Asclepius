Conformance ladder.

The conformance suite is the third-party-runnable test battery that validates a vendor integration. Three levels, each strictly extending the previous. Vendors advertise the highest level they pass; customers buy at the level their governance office requires.

L1

audit

Trustworthy log

Every wrapped inference produces an entry. Chain verifies. Bundle exports and reverifies offline. Signature key is properly held. asclepius ledger verify is green at any point in time.

target Q3 2026

L2

drift

Observable behaviour

L1 plus continuous drift telemetry on at least one model output. Override events captured with rationale. Severity transitions surfaced to operators. The model can be retired without losing replayability.

target Q4 2026

L3

evidence

Prospective trial

L2 plus ground-truth coupling on a defined fraction of inferences. Per-window comparative metrics against a published baseline. Bundle suitable for an FDA submission, signed and reproducible. The substrate becomes the trial harness.

target Q1 2027

Non-goals.

What Asclepius will not become, no matter how loudly anyone asks:

  • An EHR. We integrate with FHIR; we don't reimplement it.
  • An MLOps platform. We wrap the model, we don't train it.
  • A model serving layer. The wrapped callable is yours.
  • A SaaS-only product. The runtime is open and self-hostable. Period.
  • A post-hoc evaluation library. Asclepius emits the evidence; the library that mines it is downstream.
