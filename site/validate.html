Paste an entry on the left and click validate. The validator is a small in-page subset of JSON Schema 2020-12 (required, type, pattern, enum, minimum/maximum, additionalProperties) — sufficient for the audit-entry contract.

Why this exists. Substrate-grade openness means the contract is not a sentence in a docs page — it is a file you can execute against your inputs. This validator is the simplest possible test: does my entry shape match what the chain will accept?

For full Draft 2020-12 conformance (allOf, oneOf, $ref, format-validators), use a real validator — ajv, jsonschema, or the C++ verifier shipped with Asclepius. This page is a quick check, not a full conformance run.
