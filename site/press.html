Sample copy — pick your length

Approved phrasings. Use as-is or paraphrase; the only thing we ask is that you preserve the substantive claims. None of the below has been spun.

8 words

An open audit substrate for clinical AI.

25 words

Asclepius is an open, vendor-neutral C++ runtime that wraps any clinical-AI tool with a signed Merkle ledger, composable guardrails, drift detection, and regulator-grade evidence bundles.

paragraph

The 2026 health system runs dozens of AI models in production and hand-rolls audit, drift, consent, and evidence pipelines for every one of them. Asclepius is the substrate underneath: an open, vendor-neutral C++ runtime that wraps any clinical-AI tool with cryptographic auditability and regulator-grade evidence bundles a third party can verify offline. The trust layer cannot be a SaaS.

Tone

Editorial and clinical. Calm, not breathless. We do not call ourselves "revolutionary", do not promise "transformation", do not claim "first" without a footnote. Specific numbers preferred over adjectives. Active voice; verbs do work, adjectives don't.

Phrases we will not use: game-changer, AI-powered, next-generation, cutting-edge, seamlessly, industry-leading, disruptive. They are filler and readers know it.
