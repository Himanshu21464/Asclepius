three surfaces · 12 minutes total

Learn the substrate by running it.

Asclepius is easier to feel than to read about. The three pages below cover the full lifecycle of an audited inference — emit, hand off, inspect — and each is interactive. Open them in order. Twelve minutes from cold to confident.

01

Wrap a model in your tab.

The playground is a faithful reimplementation of the runtime in vanilla JS. Type a clinical transcript with PHI, hit wrap inference, and watch a Web-Crypto-signed Merkle ledger grow in front of you. Tamper a row; the chain goes red and tells you exactly which byte changed.

you'll learn policy chain signed entries tamper detection drift telemetry

open playground → 02

Hand it off to a verifier.

Five steps with tar, openssl, jq, and sha256sum. The exact commands a regulator runs against a real evidence bundle, with the exact expected output for each. No vendor library, no network — just the bytes.

you'll learn USTAR layout Ed25519 verify chain walking offline verification

walk the ritual → 03

Inspect a real chain.

The auditor's microscope. Drop a ledger_excerpt.jsonl from a real bundle — or generate a sample — and walk the chain entry by entry. Recompute payload hashes in the browser, see green check-marks where the chain is clean and red where it broke.

you'll learn JSONL inspection payload re-hash chain integrity in vivo
